Hello! My name is Alvin Peralta and I am a screenwriter. As a screenwriter, I tell stories through a format that is easily tunred into real films. All my life I have been interested in doing something creative. I wrote short stories in my free time, loved any english class, and I studied the wordplay I would see in the different books I'd read.

So basically, I like writing. I love writing the very thins I want to see produced by others. In the past I have written films that were produced by student film clubs. I wrote the award winning "The Friend You Can't See" during my first year attending UCR. It was the first short film I had ever written and it was the one chosen to be done by the club. I was estatic and it really gave me the confidence to pursue this career path.

I also dabbled in a bit of acting. I acted in UCR's department films "The Box" and "Terms of Service." This experience of acting gave me insight as to what it is like from another perspective to help make these scripts come to life. Using all of these different experiences to improve my writing I believe has given me a competive edge against other students. As a screenwriter, getting knowledge from different places builds better stories.
